Classic

Sisig Tofu

₱110-180

est.

More info

Sisig Tofu is a Filipino-inspired dish that offers a healthier twist on the classic pork sisig by using tofu as the main ingredient. It features crispy, pan-fried tofu cubes tossed in a savory and tangy sauce made with soy sauce, calamansi, onions, and chili peppers, often served sizzling for added flair. This flavorful, plant-based alternative is perfect as an appetizer or main dish. Affordable and easy to prepare, sisig tofu costs around ₱110-180 and serves 2-3 people as a main dish or 4-5 as an appetizer, making it a budget-friendly and delicious choice.
